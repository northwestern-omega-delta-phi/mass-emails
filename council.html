The Nu Chapter of Kappa Phi Lambda Sorority Incorporated and the Alpha Alpha Chapter of Omega Delta Phi Fraternity Incorporated would like to cordially extend an invitation for your council's chapters to participate in our annual Stroll the Yard competition. The event will allow individual chapters to showcase their strolling abilities and winners will receive cash prizes (first place winners will receive $100!) in addition to gift certificates from Chicago businesses.

There will be a registration fee of $25 for chapters who are interested in participating in the competition. All proceeds from the event will be split evenly between the two co-hosting organization's philanthropies, CARE, a leading humanitarian organization dedicated towards fighting global poverty which places special focus on working alongside poor women and equipping them with the proper resources to help entire communities, and CASA, an association that supports and promotes court-appointed volunteer advocacy so every abused or neglected child in the United States can be safe, have a permanent home and have the opportunity to thrive. For more information, please visit https://www.care.org/ or https://casaforchildren.org/.

This event is scheduled for Friday, May 10th and will take place in Lutkin Hall at Northwestern University, 700 University Place, Evanston, IL 60201. The competition will begin promptly at 7:00 PM and end around 9:30 PM. Registration will start at 6:30 PM and we encourage all organizations to arrive by this time!

If a chapter within your council would like compete in the stroll competition, please RSVP to nwukpl.president@gmail.com by Friday, April 26th. There will be a total of two rounds for each organization, and each stroll/step performance will be limited to 5 minutes. If a chapter within your council decides to participate, please also provide us with your music and a short introduction for said chapter/organization along with the RSVP.

Please do not hesitate to contact us if you have any other questions. Thank you, and we hope to see you at our event on May 10th!

Sincerely,

The Sisters of Kappa Phi Lambda Sorority Incorporated and the Brothers of Omega Delta Phi Fraternity Incorporated
